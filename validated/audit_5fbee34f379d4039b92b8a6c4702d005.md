### Title
Stale `descendants_*` Accounting After `remove_entry_and_descendants` Enables Eviction Ordering Manipulation — (File: tx-pool/src/component/pool_map.rs)

---

### Summary

In `remove_entry_and_descendants`, all transaction links are removed for the entire subtree **before** `remove_entry` is called for each entry. This causes `update_ancestors_index_key` to find no ancestors (links are already gone), leaving the `descendants_fee`, `descendants_size`, `descendants_cycles`, and `descendants_count` fields of entries **outside** the removed subtree permanently stale (inflated). The `EvictKey` computed from these stale fields makes affected entries appear more valuable than they are

### Title
Full Pool Scan on Every Block in `remove_expired` — (`tx-pool/src/pool.rs`)

### Summary

`TxPool::remove_expired` performs a full linear scan over every entry in `pool_map` on every block, regardless of how many entries are actually expired. An unprivileged transaction sender can fill the pool with many small transactions, causing this O(N) scan to execute repeatedly on each block arrival, amplifying CPU cost proportional to pool occupancy.

### Title
Keeper Can Inflate Fees via Uncapped `tx.gasprice` to Drain Subscription Balances — (File: `target_chains/ethereum/contracts/contracts/pulse/Scheduler.sol`)

---

### Summary

The `_processFeesAndPayKeeper` function in `Scheduler.sol` computes the keeper reimbursement by multiplying `(actualGasUsed + GAS_OVERHEAD)` by `tx.gasprice`, with no upper bound on `tx.gasprice`. Because `updatePriceFeeds` is permissionless, any caller acting as a keeper can submit a valid price update with an arbitrarily inflated `tx.gasprice`, extracting a disproportionate share of the subscription balance as profit from the `GAS_OVERHEAD` component.

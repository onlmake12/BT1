### Title
Miner Block Rewards Permanently Burned When Below Minimum Cell Capacity — (`verification/contextual/src/contextual_block_verifier.rs`)

---

### Summary

When a miner's total block reward falls below the minimum occupied capacity required by their lock script, the `RewardVerifier` enforces that the cellbase **must** have empty outputs. The reward is permanently burned. There is no accumulation mechanism and no alternative claim path — a direct structural analog to the eBTC `CollSurplusPool` lock-in.
